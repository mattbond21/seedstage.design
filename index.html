Hi there, I’m Matt Bond

Product Designer in San Francisco with 15 years of experience designing products and leading teams at companies like Atlassian, Asana, Dropbox, and Quizlet.

2020–2021

Quizlet

Quizlet is a consumer learning app that helps students master whatever they are studying. I was a Principal Designer leading teams for Study Tools, Discover, and Design Systems.

2017–2019

Asana

I was the Design Manager and Pillar Lead for the Adoption design team for 2.5 years. We worked on improving onboarding and core feature flows, monetization and billing flows, and enterprise scale readiness.

2015–2016

Dropbox

I was the Lead Designer and Design Manager for the File Collaboration team at Dropbox. We worked on overhauling how sharing content works, and working with VPs to set the company vision 2-3 years out.

2011–2015

Atlassian

I was a Lead Designer on a few teams including Bitbucket, HipChat, and created the Atlassian Design System.
